JNB Architecture.

JNB Architects is a modern design studio based in Chicago, specializing in sustainable architecture, urban planning, and interior design. Founded in 2012, JNB blends technology, creativity, and environmental responsibility to create impactful spaces. Notable projects include Skyline West Tower (Chicago) and Desert Bloom Pavilion (Dubai). With a global presence and a future-focused approach, JNB delivers innovative solutions for cities, communities, and clients worldwide.
